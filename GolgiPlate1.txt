Abstract: We have provided a translation of Golgi’s original paper on the mammalian hippocampus (first published in 1883 and reprinted numerous times), along with a preface on its historical context. Golgi believed that this part of the cerebral hemisphere showed best the exact relationship between nerve cells and nerve fibers, the most important problem in 19th century neuroscience.
Keywords: Hippocampus; Ammon’s horn; Dentate gyrus; Camillo Golgi; History of neuroscience; Neuron doctrine
